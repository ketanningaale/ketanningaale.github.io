Research & Projects

Dissertation, research & projects

🎓 MSc Dissertation · University of Southampton
Localisation of Autonomous Vehicles using LiDAR Sensor
Supervisors: Dr. Daniel Clark & Dr. Luis-Daniel Ibáñez Dataset: KITTI-CARLA 2023 – 2024

Explored enhancing autonomous vehicle localisation in GPS-challenged environments using LiDAR sensors. Compared traditional methods (K-Means clustering, Convex Hull, Minimum Enclosing Circle, ICP) and developed an advanced mathematical model incorporating derivative analysis and dynamic parameter adjustments. Across varied urban and rural environments, the model reduces localisation errors by up to 95%, particularly outperforming standard methods in challenging conditions — providing a more accurate and reliable alternative to GPS-based systems.
